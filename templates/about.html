MakeEdit, here you can edit your Image.

I have developed an image editing website using Flask and Bootstrap, designed to provide users with simple yet powerful tools for editing their images. The website allows users to upload images and apply various transformations, including format conversion and grayscale conversion. Users can upload their images in formats such as PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP, and then convert them to any of these formats as needed. Additionally, users can easily convert their images to grayscale, giving them a classic black-and-white look. The website features a clean and responsive design powered by Bootstrap, ensuring a smooth user experience across all devices. Image processing is handled in real-time, providing instant feedback and results to users.
